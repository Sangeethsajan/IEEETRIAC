About Us

Technical Revamping Initiative Aspired Concourse (TRIAC) is a joint ingenuity of IEEE PES,IEEE IA/IE/PELS Jt. & ComSoc Kerala Chapters. It is a two day technological extravaganza which is to be conducted at Fedral Institute of Science and Technology from 26th to 27th October 2019. It aims remodel the technical competence of IEEE Kerala Section in global level. The event compromises of Hands-on Workshop, Technical Hackathon, Project Presentation, Paper Presentation, etc.

IEEE Power & Energy Society is one of the oldest professional society of IEEE. It is focused on the scientific & engineering knowledge about electric power & energy for the betterment of world. It develops standards and empower the development of Technology software and best practices in all area of electric power & energy. It also offers various networking opportunities for its members and also allows to access to peer reviewed technical journals and educational materials that helps their members to stay up to date.

The Industry Applications Society, is a professional society of IEEE. It focuses in advancement of the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering in the development, design, manufacture and application of electrical systems, apparatus, devices and controls to the processes and equipment of industry and commerce. It promotes safe, reliable and economic installations as well as industry leadership in energy conservation and environmental, health, and safety issues. It is involved in creation of voluntary engineering standards and recommended practices.

IEEE Communication Society one out of 39 professional societies of IEEE. The focus of the society is on promoting the advancement of science technology and its applications in communication and related disciplines. It also increases the presentation and exchange of information among its members and technical community around the globe. The society maintains a high standard of professionalism and technical competency.
